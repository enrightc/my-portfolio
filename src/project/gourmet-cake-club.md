## overview

The Gourmet Cake Club is a responsive front-end website designed to showcase a curated collection of high-end cakes. The site centres around a monthly featured recipe, supported by baking tips and a newsletter sign-up to encourage ongoing user engagement.

The project focuses on clean design, strong visual presentation, and a smooth, intuitive user experience.

---

## the goal

The aim was to create a visually appealing and easy-to-navigate platform that:

- Highlights a “Cake of the Month” feature with detailed recipes
- Provides accessible baking guidance through a tips section
- Encourages users to subscribe for ongoing content

The site was designed to balance aesthetics with usability, ensuring visitors can quickly understand and explore the content.

---

## key features

- **Cake of the Month**  
  A dedicated page featuring a monthly recipe, including ingredients, method, and preparation details.

- **Baking tips section**  
  An accordion-based layout allowing users to explore common baking questions in a clean, interactive format.

- **Newsletter sign-up (modal)**  
  A non-intrusive sign-up flow accessible via navigation and call-to-action sections.

- **Responsive navigation**  
  A fully responsive navbar with a mobile-friendly hamburger menu.

- **Hero sections**  
  Distinct hero images on each page to create a visually engaging experience.

- **Call-to-action design**  
  Strategically placed prompts to encourage user interaction without disrupting the experience.

---

## design approach

The design focuses on simplicity and elegance:

- **Colour palette:** black and white for clarity and contrast
- **Typography:**
  - _Playfair Display_ for headings (adds a refined feel)
  - _Roboto_ for body text (ensures readability)
- **Imagery:** high-quality, clean compositions to reinforce a premium aesthetic

Accessibility was considered through strong contrast, readable typography, and a clear visual hierarchy.

---

## user experience

The site was built with different user types in mind:

- **First-time users** can quickly understand the purpose of the site and explore content without friction
- **Returning users** can easily locate the latest featured recipe
- **Frequent users** are encouraged to subscribe and engage more deeply

The layout prioritises clarity, with consistent structure across pages to support familiarity.

## what I learned

  was fully responsive across all screen sizes
- How to use **Bootstrap** for the first time — learning how its grid system and
  pre-built components (such as the accordion and modal) can speed up development
  and simplify responsive layouts
- The importance of responsive design and testing across devices
- How to balance visual design with accessibility
