A bit about me

I'm a junior full-stack developer with a strong interest in geospatial technology and data-driven web applications. I work with HTML, CSS, JavaScript, and Python, and have experience with frameworks including Django, React, and jQuery, as well as Leaflet for interactive mapping.

With a background in archaeology and GIS, I'm used to working with complex spatial data and thinking carefully about how it can be made easier to explore and understand. I'm especially drawn to projects that use technology to help people connect with landscapes, heritage, and place.

I'm familiar with PostgreSQL and PostGIS, with a particular focus on spatial data and the web tools that bring it to life.
